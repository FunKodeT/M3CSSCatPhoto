Cat-themed Fun Facts

Top 3 things Cats usually love:

  1. Catnip

    2. It is theorized that cats feel a sense of euphoria or extreme happiness followed by relaxation when smelling catnip. This may be due to the scent mimicking a cat's sex pheremones.

  2. Sleeping

    3. Felines have evolved to sleep for long periods throughout the day in order conserve energy hunting, chasing, and finally killing their next meal. The 'hunt instinct' is still present in domesticated cats, despite lack of need for hunting.

  3. Boxes

    4. Cats love boxes, and though there are many reasons, the primary one is they are confined and enclosed spaces. This instinct is driven by wild felines commonly using enclosed spaces for hiding, hunting, safety, and warmth.

Top 3 things Cats usually hate:

  1. Dirty litter boxes

    2. Cat's are very concerned with cleanliness, and are just as averse to using a dirty toiletry area as humans are.

  2. Staring

    3. The reality of this is quite simple. Direct eye contact, specifically for extended times, is viewed by the cat as a threatening gesture.

  3. Too much or too little attention

    4. A cat is a largely independent creature, largely preferring solace and reliance on self. However, they are also social creatures to an extent. This largely results in cats only wishing to have attention given to them on their own terms.

Common Misconceptions:

  • Cats love lasagna and milk.

    • Cat's are lactose intolerant, and struggle to digest milk-based products such as cheese, milk, and lasagna.

  • Cats really like laser pointers.

    • You can damage your Cat's retina's through the use of a laser pointer. They look like they enjoy it, but it can cause permanent damage due to the sensitity of their eyes.

  • All cats are evil.

    • Each cat has a unique personality, just like dogs. This can result in some being lovely, and others being perceived as 'evil'.
